01. Data Security & Sovereignty

FortressVote employs end-to-end zero-knowledge proofs (ZKP) to ensure your identity remains private while verifying your eligibility. 02. Immutable Record Keeping

Once registered, your intent to vote is recorded on a private, permissioned ledger. This record is cryptographically signed and cannot be altered, spoofed, or removed after the audit window closes. Your privacy is maintained through hashed identifiers.

03. Voter Responsibility

You attest that the information provided is accurate and that you are the sole operator of this authenticated session. Any attempt to use duplicate identities or circumvent residency checks will result in immediate disqualification and a security flag on your citizen profile.

04. Audit & Verification

You will receive a unique, anonymous cryptographic receipt upon completion. This receipt allows you to verify that your registration was correctly counted in the public audit without revealing your vote or identity.
